You are stuck in Boo's Mansion.
The objective of the game is to find your way out.
Move from room to room by correctly answering questions to search for the escape room.
However, if you answer more than 4 questions incorrectly you will be stuck in Boo's Mansion
FOREVERRR!!!
The character you choose dictates the difficulty:
Mario = Hard
Luigi = Medium
Kirby = Easy
Here is the map of the game, the x marks where you will begin.
The escape room is any one of the other 15 rooms
If you choose a location outside of the map you will immediately lose the game!
